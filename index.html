About Cowgirl

Cowgirl is founded by Sofie Bergh in the heart of Sweden: Dalarna. With knowledge of embroidery handicraft, Sofie started making her own clothing. In time, the idea to start Cowgirl emerged.

Today Cowgirl is an established clothing brand in Sweden.
